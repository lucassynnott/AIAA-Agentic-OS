## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Twitter is the fastest platform for building B2B authority. Combining content with DM outreach creates a powerful inbound/outbound hybrid.

**Why Twitter Works for B2B**:
- Decision makers are active
- Content spreads quickly
- DMs are accessible
- Authority builds fast

## 2. PROFILE OPTIMIZATION

### Essential Elements

**Name**: Real name (searchable)
**Handle**: Professional, memorable
**Photo**: Professional headshot
**Banner**: Value proposition or brand
**Bio**: Who you help + what result

### Bio Formula

```
I help [avatar] [achieve result].
[Credibility/proof]
[CTA or what you share]
```

**Example**:
```
I help B2B founders book 50+ sales calls/month.
Built 3 agencies to $100K+/mo.
Sharing cold email & lead gen tactics daily.
```

## 3. CONTENT STRATEGY

### Content Types

**Threads** (educational deep-dives):
- How-to content
- Frameworks
- Case studies
- Best for growth

**Single Tweets** (quick value):
- Insights
- Hot takes
- Observations
- Best for engagement

**Replies** (visibility):
- Add value to big accounts
- Be early
- Say something smart

### Thread Structure

1. **Hook**: Stops the scroll
2. **Promise**: What they'll learn
3. **Body**: The value (5-10 tweets)
4. **Conclusion**: Summary + CTA
5. **Engagement**: Ask for RT/follow

### Posting Frequency

- 3-10 tweets per day
- 1-2 threads per week
- Consistent timing
- Heavy on replies

---

## 4. GROWTH TACTICS

### Engagement Strategy

- Reply to bigger accounts
- Add value (not "great post!")
- Be early on viral tweets
- Build relationships

### Thread Hooks

**Proven Formulas**:
- "I spent [X] learning [Y]. Here's what actually works:"
- "[X] lessons from [achievement/experience]:"
- "The [topic] playbook that got us [result]:"
- "Most people [do X]. Here's what works instead:"

### Virality Factors

- Actionable content
- Contrarian takes
- Specific numbers
- Clear formatting
- Strong hooks

## 5. DM OUTREACH

### The Warm DM Approach

1. Engage with their content first
2. Connect through mutual interests
3. Provide value before asking
4. Natural transition to conversation

### DM Sequence

**DM 1 (After engaging)**:
```
Hey [Name], been enjoying your content on [topic].

Your point about [specific thing] really resonated.

What's your main focus right now?
```

**DM 2 (If they respond)**:
```
Makes sense. [Brief response to their answer]

I actually work with [similar people] on [what you do]. Recently helped [brief result].

Would you be interested in seeing how that might apply to your situation?
```

**DM 3 (If interested)**:
```
Great! Let's hop on a quick 15-min call.

What's your calendar look like this week? Or here's mine: [link]
```

### DM Best Practices

- Personalize every message
- Reference their content
- Keep messages short
- No pitch in first message
- Add value before asking
