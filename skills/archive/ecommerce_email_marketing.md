# E-Commerce Email Marketing

## Overview
Email marketing is the highest-ROI channel for e-commerce businesses. This skill covers campaign strategies, automation flows, segmentation, and technical setup for e-commerce email marketing agencies.

## Core Revenue Flows

### 1. Welcome Series
- New subscriber nurture sequence
- Brand introduction and value proposition
- First purchase incentive
- Set expectations for future emails

### 2. Abandoned Cart
- Trigger when cart is left incomplete
- 3-email series over 24-48 hours
- Reminder + urgency + social proof
- Discount on final email (optional)

### 3. Post-Purchase
- Thank you + order confirmation
- Shipping updates
- Cross-sell/upsell opportunities
- Review request sequence

### 4. Browse Abandonment
- Triggered when viewing products without adding to cart
- Product recommendations based on browsing
- Social proof and urgency

### 5. Win-Back Campaigns
- Re-engage inactive customers
- Special offers or new product announcements
- "We miss you" messaging
- Sunset inactive subscribers

### 6. VIP/Loyalty Programs
- Reward high-value customers
- Exclusive access to new products
- Special discounts and perks
- Referral incentives

## Campaign Types

### Promotional Campaigns
- Sale announcements
- New product launches

### Educational/Content
- How-to guides
- Product education
- Customer stories

### Transactional
- Order confirmations
- Shipping notifications
- Delivery confirmations
- Return/refund updates

## Segmentation Strategy

### Key Segments
- **By Engagement**: Active, lapsing, inactive
- **By Purchase History**: First-time, repeat, VIP
- **By Product Interest**: Category browsers, specific product viewers
- **By Lifecycle Stage**: Subscriber, customer, advocate
- **By Demographics**: Location, age, preferences
- **By Purchase Frequency**: High, medium, low

### Personalization Tactics
- Dynamic product recommendations
- Location-based offers
- Behavior-triggered content
- Name and purchase history references
- Predictive product suggestions

## Technical Deliverability

### Domain Setup
- Authenticate sending domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Use subdomain for marketing emails
- Maintain separate IP for transactional vs promotional

### List Hygiene
- Regular list cleaning
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Sunset unengaged subscribers
- Double opt-in for quality
- Easy unsubscribe process

### Engagement Optimization
- Optimize send times by timezone
- Test subject lines and preview text
- Mobile-first design
- Fast-loading images
- Clear CTAs

## Copywriting Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- 40-50 characters ideal
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Personalization tokens
- A/B test variants
- Avoid spam trigger words

### Email Body
- Scannable format (short paragraphs, bullets)
- Compelling hero image
- Clear value proposition
- Single primary CTA
- Mobile-responsive design
- Brand-consistent visuals

### Call-to-Action
- Action-oriented language
- Contrast color buttons
- Multiple placement options (above/below fold)
- Urgency or scarcity when appropriate

## Design Considerations
- Mobile-first approach (60%+ opens on mobile)
- Simple, clean layouts
- Brand consistency
- High-quality product images
- Readable fonts (16px+ for body)
- Sufficient white space
- Accessible color contrast

## Metrics & KPIs

### Primary Metrics
- **Open Rate**: 15-25% average for e-commerce
- **Click-Through Rate**: 2-5% average
- **Conversion Rate**: 1-3% email to purchase
- **Revenue Per Email**: Track per campaign
- **List Growth Rate**: Monthly net growth
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Keep under 0.5%

### Secondary Metrics
- Bounce rate (keep under 2%)
- Spam complaint rate (keep under 0.1%)
- Forward/share rate
- Time spent reading
- Device breakdown (mobile vs desktop)
