# Maximize Show Rates

## Purpose
Implement proven copywriting strategies and psychological triggers throughout the booking-to-call journey to achieve 70%+ show rates.

## When to Use
- Calendar show rates below 60%
- Setting up new booking funnel
- Improving existing confirmation sequences
- Testing new reminder messaging
- Scaling calendar operations

## Core Philosophy

**Show rates are won or lost between booking and call time.**

Most businesses focus on getting the booking. Elite operators focus on what happens AFTER the booking.

Your job: Increase commitment, build urgency, and make showing up the path of least resistance.

## The Show Rate Formula

```
Show Rate = (Commitment + Urgency + Clarity) - (Friction + Time Decay)
```

**Commitment:** How invested they feel
**Urgency:** Cost of not showing up
**Clarity:** How easy it is to show up
**Friction:** Obstacles to showing up
**Time Decay:** Enthusiasm fading over time

Maximize the first three, minimize the last two.

## Target Show Rates by Funnel

```
Cold Traffic (Ads ‚Üí Booking): 50-60%
Warm Traffic (Webinar ‚Üí Booking): 60-75%
Hot Traffic (Application ‚Üí Booking): 75-85%
Referral Traffic: 80-90%
```

If you're below these benchmarks, your confirmation and reminder sequences need work.

### Component 2: Pre-Call Assignments

Giving them homework increases commitment exponentially.

**Why It Works:**
- Effort = Investment (sunk cost fallacy)
- Gives you more data
- Warms them up
- Filters out non-serious

#### Assignment Option 1: Detailed Questionnaire

```

"Before Our Call, Complete This Quick Questionnaire"

[Button: "Fill Out Now" ‚Üí Link to Form]

---

2. What's currently preventing you from hitting that goal?

6. What's the biggest concern you have about working with someone like us?
```

### Component 5: Urgency Building Copy

Sprinkle these psychological triggers throughout:

#### Scarcity:

```
"I only take [X] calls per week - glad you got a spot."

"This time slot was the last one available this week."

"If you need to reschedule, do it soon - my calendar fills up fast."
```

#### Opportunity Cost:

```
"Every day you wait to solve [problem] costs you $[X]."

"You've been dealing with this for [timeline]. Let's fix it."

"The sooner we talk, the sooner you can [desired outcome]."
```

#### Social Proof:

```
"I just helped [Similar Client] go from [A] to [B] - excited to do the same for you."

"Last week alone I had [X] calls with [industry] businesses."

"[X]% of people who take this call end up working with us."
```

#### Preparation:

```
"I'm already prepping for our call based on your answers."

"I've been thinking about your challenge - I have some ideas."

"I'm setting aside the full [30] minutes to focus on YOUR situation."
```

This builds reciprocity - they feel obligated to show up because you're investing time.

### Component 6: Social Commitment

Make them publicly commit (to themselves):

#### In Confirmation Email:

```
"Quick Question: Are you committed to showing up on [Day]?

Reply YES if you're serious about solving [problem].

(If not, no hard feelings - just reschedule so someone else can take the spot.)"
```

When they reply "YES," they've publicly committed. Harder to no-show.

#### In Questionnaire:

```
"On a scale of 1-10, how committed are you to solving [problem]?

1 = Just browsing
10 = I need this solved ASAP

[1-10 scale]

If you selected below 7, why did you book this call?"
```

This forces self-reflection. Low scorers often self-disqualify (good - saves your time).

## Show Rate Copy Templates by Funnel

### For Cold Traffic (Ads ‚Üí Booking):

Higher friction, lower commitment. Need MORE touchpoints.

```
Confirmation Page: Emphasize value heavily
Email Sequence: 5 emails (immediate, 3-day, 24hr, 2hr, 15min)
SMS Sequence: 4 texts (immediate, 24hr, 2hr, 15min)
Pre-Call Assignment: Mandatory questionnaire
```

### For Warm Traffic (Webinar ‚Üí Booking):

Medium commitment. Need value reminder.

```
Confirmation Page: Reference webinar content
Email Sequence: 4 emails (immediate, 24hr, 2hr, 15min)
SMS Sequence: 3 texts (immediate, 24hr, 15min)
Pre-Call Assignment: Optional video or case study
```

### For Hot Traffic (Application ‚Üí Booking):

High commitment. Need minimal reminders.

```
Confirmation Page: Simple and clear
Email Sequence: 3 emails (immediate, 24hr, 15min)
SMS Sequence: 2 texts (24hr, 15min)
Pre-Call Assignment: Brief recap of application
```

## Advanced Show Rate Strategies

### Strategy 1: The Deposit

For very high-ticket or to filter serious buyers:

```
"To secure your spot, there's a $[X] refundable deposit.

This ensures you're serious (and so am I).

If you show up, you get it back.
If you don't show, it's forfeited."
```

Show rates with deposits: 95%+

### Strategy 2: The Penalty

Less aggressive than deposit:

```
"If you can't make it, please reschedule at least 24 hours in advance.

No-shows without notice will not be rescheduled."
```

Creates accountability without money.

### Strategy 3: The Voice Note

1-2 days before call, send personal voice note (Loom, voice text):

```
Come with questions. This is going to be valuable.

See you [Day]!
```

Highly personal. Massively increases show rates.
