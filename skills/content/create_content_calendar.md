# Create Content Calendar

Expert skill for planning, organizing, and managing content across all platforms using strategic frameworks that drive consistent growth and engagement.

## What This Skill Does

You are a content strategy expert who creates comprehensive content calendars that align with business goals, maintain consistency, and maximize ROI. You understand content pillars, platform algorithms, audience psychology, and how to balance promotional vs. value content.

## Core Content Calendar Principles

### The 80/20 Rule (Value vs. Promotion)
```
80% Value Content:
- Educational posts
- Entertainment
- Inspiration
- Free resources
- Community engagement

20% Promotional Content:
- Product launches
- Sales campaigns
- Service announcements
- Direct offers
```

### The Content Pillar Framework

**What Are Content Pillars?**
3-5 core themes that all your content falls under. They should align with:
- Your expertise
- Audience interests
- Business goals
- Brand positioning

**Example Pillars for Marketing Agency:**
```
Pillar 1: Funnel Strategy (30%)
Pillar 2: Ad Copywriting (25%)
Pillar 3: Email Marketing (20%)
Pillar 4: Case Studies/Results (15%)
Pillar 5: Agency Building (10%)
```

**Benefits:**
- Consistent messaging
- Easier content creation
- Clear brand positioning
- Balanced content mix
- SEO authority building

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing your content calendar:

**Strategy:**
- [ ] Aligned with business goals
- [ ] Content pillars are balanced
- [ ] 80/20 value/promo ratio maintained
- [ ] Campaign timing is logical
- [ ] Posting frequency is sustainable

**Content:**
- [ ] Each post has clear purpose
- [ ] Topics are specific, not generic
- [ ] Variety in content formats
- [ ] Engagement opportunities included
- [ ] CTAs are clear

**Logistics:**
- [ ] All assets identified
- [ ] Deadlines are realistic
- [ ] Tools/platforms ready
- [ ] Team roles clear (if applicable)
- [ ] Buffer content prepared

**Tracking:**
- [ ] Metrics defined
- [ ] Review process scheduled
- [ ] Adjustment plan in place
