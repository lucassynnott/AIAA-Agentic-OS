# SKILL BIBLE: Agency Self-Marketing

> **Purpose**: How agencies should market themselves to attract clients.
> **Word Count Target**: 2,000+
> **Last Updated**: 2026-01-02

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The cobbler's children have no shoes. Many agencies are great at marketing clients but terrible at marketing themselves. Fix this.

**The Agency Marketing Stack**:
1. Positioning & messaging
2. Website/landing page
3. Content marketing
4. Outbound (cold email/LinkedIn)
5. Referrals

---

## 2. POSITIONING FIRST

### Before Marketing

Define:
- Who you serve (specific)
- What you do (clear)
- What results you deliver (provable)
- Why you're different (unique)

### Your One-Liner

"We help [specific audience] [achieve result] through [mechanism]."

Example: "We help B2B SaaS companies generate 50+ qualified leads/month through cold email."

---

## 3. WEBSITE/LANDING PAGE

### Homepage Essentials

**Above fold**:
- Headline: Who you help + result
- Subheadline: How/what
- CTA: Book call
- Trust indicators

**Below fold**:
- Problem you solve
- Your process
- Case studies (2-3)
- Testimonials
- FAQ
- Final CTA

### Keep It Simple

- One clear CTA
- Focused messaging
- No corporate speak
- Mobile optimized

---

## 4. CONTENT MARKETING

### Content Strategy

**Pillars**:
1. Your service area expertise
2. Client results/case studies
3. Industry insights
4. Behind-the-scenes

### Platform Focus

Pick 1-2:
- LinkedIn (B2B primary)
- Twitter (B2B secondary)
- YouTube (long-term authority)

### Content That Converts

- Educational (how-to)
- Case studies (proof)
- Hot takes (engagement)
- Personal stories (trust)

---

## 5. OUTBOUND

### Cold Email for Agencies

- Target your ICP
- Lead with value/insight
- Share relevant case study
- Soft CTA (call or reply)

### LinkedIn for Agencies

- Optimize profile
- Post valuable content
- Engage with prospects
- DM sequence after connection

### Consistency

- Outbound daily/weekly
- 20-50 outreach per day
- Follow-up religiously

---

## 6. REFERRAL SYSTEMS

### Asking Clients

- Ask after wins
- Make it easy
- Offer incentive (optional)

### Partner Referrals

- Find complementary services
- Exchange referrals
- Formal or informal
