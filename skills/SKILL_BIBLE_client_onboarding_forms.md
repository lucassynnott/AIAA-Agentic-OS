# SKILL BIBLE: Client Onboarding Forms & Processes

> **Purpose**: Creating effective intake forms and kickoff processes for new clients.
> **Word Count Target**: 2,000+
> **Last Updated**: 2026-01-02

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Onboarding sets the tone. A smooth onboarding = happy clients. Gather everything you need upfront so you can hit the ground running.

**Onboarding Goals**:
1. Collect all required information
2. Set clear expectations
3. Build trust and rapport
4. Create accountability
5. Achieve quick wins

---

## 2. ONBOARDING FORM DESIGN

### What to Collect

**Business Info**:
- Company name, website
- Industry/niche
- Target audience
- Competitors

**Access & Credentials**:
- Ad accounts
- Analytics
- CRM
- Social accounts
- Email platform

**Goals & Context**:
- Primary goals
- KPIs/metrics
- Past performance
- What's been tried

**Assets**:
- Brand guidelines
- Logos, images
- Content/copy
- Case studies

**Communication**:
- Preferred channels
- Key contacts
- Meeting preferences

### Form Tools

- Typeform (beautiful, branching)
- Google Forms (free, simple)
- Notion (flexible)
- GHL forms (integrated)

### Form Best Practices

- Section by topic
- Clear instructions
- Required vs optional marked
- Progress indicator
- Save and continue

---

## 3. KICKOFF CALL STRUCTURE

### Agenda (60-90 min)

**1. Introductions (5 min)**
- Who's on call
- Roles and responsibilities

**2. Goals Review (10 min)**
- Confirm objectives
- Define success metrics

**3. Process Overview (15 min)**
- How you work
- Communication cadence
- What to expect

**4. Information Review (20 min)**
- Walk through onboarding form
- Clarify anything unclear
- Identify gaps

**5. Timeline & Milestones (10 min)**
- What happens when
- First deliverables
- Review schedule

**6. Communication Setup (10 min)**
- Slack/channel setup
- Meeting cadence
- Reporting schedule

**7. Questions (10 min)**
- Address concerns
- Clarify anything

**8. Next Steps (5 min)**
- Immediate actions
- Who does what

---

## 4. POST-KICKOFF

### Same Day

- Send recap email
- List all action items
- Confirm next meeting
- Share access info collected

### Week 1

- Complete account access
- Initial audit/analysis
- Set up tracking
- Begin work
