# SKILL BIBLE: Client Reporting & Dashboards

> **Purpose**: Creating effective reports and dashboards for client communication.
> **Word Count Target**: 2,000+
> **Last Updated**: 2026-01-02

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Reports demonstrate value and build trust. Bad reporting leads to confused clients and churn. Good reporting makes renewals easy.

**Reporting Principles**:
- Show results, not just activity
- Connect to their goals
- Make it easy to understand
- Be proactive, not reactive

---

## 2. REPORT TYPES

### Weekly Update

- Quick status
- Key metrics
- What was done
- What's next

### Monthly Report

- Comprehensive performance
- Goal progress
- Analysis and insights
- Recommendations

### Quarterly Review

- Big picture progress
- Strategic assessment
- Next quarter planning
- Expansion opportunities

---

## 3. REPORT STRUCTURE

### Monthly Report Template

**1. Executive Summary**
- Key wins
- Overall performance
- Quick takeaways

**2. KPI Dashboard**
- Primary metrics
- Goal vs actual
- Trend direction

**3. What We Did**
- Activities completed
- Deliverables produced
- Tests run

**4. Results**
- Metric details
- Before/after
- Attribution

**5. Analysis**
- What worked
- What didn't
- Why

**6. Recommendations**
- What to do next
- Opportunities
- Required actions

**7. Next Month**
- Planned activities
- Goals

---

## 4. METRICS TO INCLUDE

### Lead Generation

- Leads generated
- Cost per lead
- Lead quality score
- Pipeline value

### Ads/Traffic

- Impressions, clicks, CTR
- CPC, CPM
- Conversions
- ROAS

### Email Marketing

- Emails sent
- Open rate, click rate
- Revenue attributed
- List growth

### Sales

- Calls booked
- Show rate
- Close rate
- Revenue

---

## 5. DASHBOARD DESIGN

### Design Principles

- Visual hierarchy
- Most important first
- Easy to scan
- Mobile accessible
- Real-time when possible

### Tools

- Google Data Studio (Looker)
- Databox
- AgencyAnalytics
- Klipfolio
- Custom (Notion, Sheets)

### Must-Haves

- Primary KPI prominently displayed
- Goal tracking
- Trend visualization
- Date filtering
- Comparison periods

---

## 6. COMMUNICATION

### Delivery

- Consistent schedule
- Same format each time
- Accompany with summary
- Offer to discuss

### Narrative

Don't just show numbers—tell the story:
- What happened
- Why it matters
- What we're doing about it

### Handling Bad Numbers

- Be proactive (don't hide)
- Explain what happened
- Show the plan to fix
- Provide context
