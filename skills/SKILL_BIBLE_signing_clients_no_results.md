# SKILL BIBLE: How to Sign Clients With No Clients or Results (Daniel Fazio Method)

## Executive Summary

This skill bible documents the complete system for signing clients when you have zero clients and zero results. The chicken-and-egg problem solved: the client comes first, then results, then easier clients. The key insight: use yourself as your free first client, de-risk for prospects, and understand that difficulty decreases with each case study you acquire.

**Key Quote:** "It is extremely difficult to get your first client because you don't have results. But when you finally get that client and you don't give them results, it will always be the most difficult it can be for the rest of your life."

---

## Section 1: The Chicken and Egg Reality

### The Sequence

```
Hard → Sign First Client
        ↓
Get Results for Client
        ↓
Easier → Sign Second Client
        ↓
Get More Results
        ↓
Even Easier → Sign Third Client
        ↓
[Continues getting easier]
```

### Real-World Example

**Xavier's short-form content agency:**

**Day 1-8:** Extremely difficult
- Found dating coach
- Made 5 shorts from existing content
- Sent short every other day until response
- Got email, sent personalized Loom
- Sent two proposals
- Back and forth
- Sent invoice, got paid

**Day 9:** Extremely easy
- Posted first client results: 700,000 views
- Got first inbound call booked through landing page

**What changed?** The RESULTS.

### The Fundamental Truth

**If you don't deliver client results, it will ALWAYS be hard to get clients.**

This is the primary issue with most struggling businesses:
- Want to sign all the clients
- Want to make all the money
- Deliver zero of the results

## Section 2: The Self-Client Principle

### Your Free First Client

**You have one free client requiring:**
- Zero dollars
- Zero effort to sign
- YOURSELF

### The Hypocrisy Check

**Question:** Are you doing what you're selling?

| Selling | But Are You... |
|---------|---------------|
| Email marketing | Running your own email list? |
| Short-form content | Posting short-form content yourself? |
| Cold email lead gen | Signing clients via cold email yourself? |
| 100K TikTok followers | Have 100K TikTok followers yourself? |

### The Logical Problem

**If you're selling short-form content but not using it yourself:**
- You clearly aren't confident in it
- Why would a prospect be confident in you?
- You're asking them to bet on something you won't bet on

**Fix:** Do the thing yourself first.

## Section 3: De-Risking for Prospects

### The Core Psychology

**Humans are loss-averse:**
- Losing $100 hurts MORE than gaining $100 feels good
- Risk = #1 blocker to sales
- Your job = Remove risk

### Two Ways to De-Risk

**1. Guarantee**
- Money-back guarantee
- Performance basis (only paid on results)

**2. Perception**
- Results from yourself (internal proof)
- Results from clients (social proof)

### The Proof Priority

| Type | Description | Power |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Client case studies | Third-party validation | Highest |
| Your own results | First-party proof | High |
| Guarantee | Risk transfer | Medium |
| Nothing | Maximum risk | Lowest conversion |

## Section 4: The Four Conversion Elements

### Element 1: Claims

**What you're actually selling:**
- NOT: "Short-form content"
- NOT: "Email marketing"
- YES: "The gap between current state and end state"

**Formula:**
```
Current State → [Your Service = The Gap] → End State
```

**Examples:**
- "I will close $50,000 in new business using your email list in 60 days without running fake scarcity promos"
- "I will script, edit, and produce your next 8 YouTube videos and guarantee 3x more views or you don't pay"

**Structure:**
```
[Quantifiable Promise] + [End Result] + [Without Objection They Have]
```

---

### Element 2: Proof

**Purpose:** Convert the next subsection of population who need evidence.

**Forms of proof:**
1. Interview with client explaining results
2. Screenshots of results (client's or yours)
3. Selfie testimonial videos from clients
4. Independent case study walkthroughs (Loom style)

**Required structure:**
```
Current State → Gap (What You Did) → End State
```

**Every piece of social proof is a STORY:**
- Where they were
- What was executed
- Where they ended up

---

### Element 3: Competency

**Purpose:** Convert the most logical prospects who want to see you're a master.

**How to demonstrate:**
- Explainer videos
- Teach stories
- Walk-through videos
- Process breakdowns

**Why this works for B2B:**
- B2B buyers are busy
- They see your content
- Think: "I could do this..."
- Then: "But I don't have time"
- Decision: "I'll just hire this guy"

**Why it doesn't work for B2C:**
- B2C buyers are often broke
- They see your content
- Think: "I'll just do this myself"
- Do it themselves (or try to)

---

### Element 4: Consistency

**Purpose:** Convert the 99% who aren't ready to buy RIGHT NOW.

### The Conversion Window

**Reality:** Not everyone is ready to purchase right now.

**Reasons:**
- Grandma died
- Someone quit
- Girlfriend broke up
- Random life stuff

**Concept:** Every person has an average time until their "conversion window" opens.

### Permanent Association

**Strategy:** Talk about your thing so consistently that you BECOME the thing.

**Example:** @ColdEmailWizard on Twitter
- 116,000 followers
- Topic: Cold email
- Result: People think "cold email" = think of him

**How it works:**
1. Post about X for a year straight
2. YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, etc.
3. Audience permanently associates X with you
4. When their conversion window opens, they buy from you

## Section 5: The Beginner Strategy

### If You Have Zero Clients

**Step 1:** Become your own case study
- Do the thing you're selling
- Get results for yourself
- Document everything

**Step 2:** Offer massive de-risk
- Work on performance basis (paid only after results)
- OR money-back guarantee
- No upfront cost to them

**Step 3:** Sign first client
- Understand it will be HARD
- That's normal
- Push through

**Step 4:** Over-deliver results
- Put in extreme effort
- Get them the best results possible
- This is your ticket to easier future clients

**Step 5:** Document the case study
- Interview them
- Get screenshots
- Create proof assets

**Step 6:** Use proof to sign next client
- Show what you did
- This one will be easier
- Repeat

## Section 7: Action Plan

### Phase 1: Build Internal Proof (Week 1-2)

- [ ] Identify what you want to sell
- [ ] Apply it to yourself/your business
- [ ] Document results meticulously
- [ ] Create before/after proof

### Phase 2: Create Claim (Week 2)

- [ ] Define specific quantifiable result
- [ ] Identify common objection to address
- [ ] Write claim using formula
- [ ] Test claim messaging

### Phase 3: Add De-Risk (Week 2)

- [ ] Choose: Performance basis OR guarantee
- [ ] Define exact terms
- [ ] Add to offer

### Phase 4: Start Outreach (Week 3+)

- [ ] Build target list
- [ ] Create outreach sequences
- [ ] Send consistently
- [ ] Accept that first client is HARD

### Phase 5: Over-Deliver (Ongoing)

- [ ] Go above and beyond for first clients
- [ ] Document every win
- [ ] Create case study immediately
- [ ] Use to make next client easier
