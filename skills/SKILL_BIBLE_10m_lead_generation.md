### Conversion Mechanism #1: Service-Based Lead Magnet (The Best)

**Definition:**
Something that requires labor - something people would ordinarily pay for, but you give for free.

**Why It Works (The Triple Effect):**

**1. Invokes Reciprocity**
"It multiplies the chance they buy from you by roughly 10,000% to 35,000%. Actually, it works that substantially well. It's just reciprocity.

If you do something good for someone, if you do something nice for someone, they feel like they have to repay you. This is why content marketing works - if you have very valuable content, people feel like they have to pay you back."

**2. Proves Competence**
"If you make a service-based lead magnet and you tell someone you're going to make them a free ad creative, and the ad creative isn't good, the campaign is obviously not going to work. But if it is good, then you have immediately proved competence."

**3. Accelerates Know/Like/Trust**
Shortens the typical 6-12 month relationship building to days/weeks.

**2. Lead Magnet Funnel**

**Structure:**
- Ad → Free download/guide/case study → Email sequence → Call booking

**When It Works:**
- Warm traffic (retargeting)
- Organic traffic
- People already familiar with you

**When It Doesn't Work:**
- Cold traffic from ads (usually)

---

**3. Self-Liquidating Funnel (SLO)**

**Structure:**
- Ad → Low-ticket product ($28-97) → Upsell to high-ticket

**Fazio's SLO Campaign:**

**Offer:** Case study on "How we send 10,000 cold emails per day and grew a new business to $1M ARR in 3 months"

**Two Versions:**
- $28 paid version
- Free version

**Result:** 8X ROAS

**The Math of SLO:**

**Scenario 1: Direct VSL Funnel**
- $1,000 ad spend
- $300 cost per call
- 3.33 calls booked

**Scenario 2: SLO Funnel**
- $1,000 ad spend
- 70% ad spend returned from low-ticket sales ($700)
- Effective ad spend: $300
- $90 cost per call (because you recouped $700)
- 11 calls booked

**Or Even Better:**
- $1,000 ad spend
- $1,000 returned from low-ticket sales
- 2 calls booked
- Cost per call: $0 (free leads)

**Fazio's Note:**
"Figuring this out and getting this to work properly - there's no automatic, you just make this much. It's very difficult and you have to make sure you get it done correctly. It's not as easy as it looks. 99% of people are going to lose money when they start ads."

---

### Why 99% Fail at Ads

**The Misconception:**
"When people say 'get really good at ads,' what they actually mean by that is: The ad itself is so little of the actual performance of the ad campaign."

**What Actually Determines Success:**

| Component | Importance |
|-----------|------------|
| The offer | 40% |
| The VSL/landing page copy | 30% |
| Email follow-up sequences | 15% |
| Sales call performance | 10% |

**Fazio's Advice:**
"There's so many components that go into this, and this is why it's so difficult and why I tell people not to start with ads. It's not just 'spin up an ad and then you make money.'

**The Concept:**
"There's this concept that there's an intersection between somebody whose conversion window is elapsing and you exist in front of them. Those need to intersect. It's very important that that happens."

**Visualization:**

```
Person's Buying Journey:
├── Month 1-2: Problem awareness (not ready to buy)
```

**Without Aggregators:**

- Miss 95% of potential buyers

**With Aggregators:**
- You email them 5X/week
- You appear in their YouTube feed
- You show up in their Twitter timeline
- When their conversion window DOES open → You're top-of-mind
- Capture 50-80% of potential buyers
