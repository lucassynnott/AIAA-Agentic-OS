## Common Mistakes

**1. Imposter Syndrome and Underconfidence**
- Believing you need extensive technical background before starting
- Undervaluing your services due to perceived lack of expertise
- Waiting for perfect knowledge before taking action

**2. Poor Niche and Market Selection**
- Targeting AI-aware companies that may build solutions internally
- Trying to serve too many industries without specialization
- Competing on price rather than value and results

**3. Technical and Process Errors**
- Using generic prompts without visual references or context
- Skipping client discovery and mood boarding processes
- Building complex solutions when simple ones would suffice

**4. Business Development Mistakes**
- Cold outreach without warm introductions or referrals
- Focusing on features and technology rather than business outcomes
- Scaling too quickly before mastering core operations

**5. Operational Failures**
- Not documenting processes and successful methodologies
- Failing to build systems that can be delegated and scaled
- Neglecting client results tracking and case study development

**6. Pricing and Positioning Errors**
- Underpricing services relative to value delivered
- Not raising prices as experience and results improve
- Competing as commodity service rather than strategic partner
