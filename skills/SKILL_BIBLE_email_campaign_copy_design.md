## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Email campaigns are scheduled broadcasts to your list. Unlike flows (automated), campaigns are intentional, timed sends.

**Campaign Equation**:
```
Revenue = List Size × Open Rate × Click Rate × Conversion Rate × AOV
```

**Optimal Frequency**: 3-4 campaigns per week for e-commerce.

## 3. SUBJECT LINE MASTERY

### Subject Line Rules

- Under 50 characters ideal
- Clear benefit or curiosity
- Test constantly
- Personalization when appropriate

### Formulas

**Curiosity**:
- "The [thing] nobody talks about"
- "Why [common belief] is wrong"

**Urgency**:
- "Last chance: [offer] ends tonight"
- "[X] hours left"

**Benefit**:
- "Get [result] in [timeframe]"
- "How to [achieve outcome]"

**Personal**:
- "[Name], you left something..."
- "Picked for you"

### Preview Text

- Extends subject line
- 40-90 characters
- Don't repeat subject
- Add context or intrigue

---

## 5. COPY FRAMEWORKS

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

**P**: State the problem
**A**: Make it worse
**S**: Present solution

### AIDA

**A**: Attention (hook)
**I**: Interest (relevant info)
**D**: Desire (benefits)
**A**: Action (CTA)

### The Story Email

1. Hook with situation
2. Conflict/challenge
3. Resolution
4. Bridge to offer
5. CTA

---

## 6. EMAIL DESIGN

### Design Principles

- Mobile-first (60%+ on mobile)
- Single column layout
- High contrast
- Clear hierarchy
- Fast loading

### Visual Elements

**Images**:
- Optimized file size
- Alt text always
- Don't rely solely on images

**CTAs**:
- Button format (not just links)
- High contrast color
- Clear action text
- One primary CTA

**White Space**:
- Don't cram content
- Let elements breathe
- Improve readability

### Typography

- Body: 16px minimum
- Headlines: Clear hierarchy
- Line height: 1.4-1.6
- Readable fonts

---

## 7. A/B TESTING

### What to Test

**High Impact**:
1. Subject lines
2. Send time
3. Offer/discount

**Medium Impact**:
4. CTA copy
5. Email length
6. Images

### Testing Rules

- Test one variable at a time
- 1,000+ per variant for significance
- Run full 24-48 hours
- Document results

## 8. CAMPAIGN CALENDAR

### Weekly Template

**Monday**: Content/educational
**Wednesday**: Promotional
**Friday**: Promotional or social proof
**Saturday/Sunday**: Optional light send

### Planning

- Plan 2-4 weeks ahead
- Align with promotions
- Mix content types
- Avoid fatigue
