# INOVATIV Brand Voice Guidelines

## Overall Tone
**Casual, Friendly, and Funny**

## Voice Characteristics

### Casual
- Use conversational language, not corporate jargon
- Write like you're talking to a friend who happens to run a successful e-commerce brand
- Contractions are your friend (we're, you'll, don't)
- Skip the stiff formalities

### Friendly
- Be approachable and warm
- Use "you" and "we" to create connection
- Show genuine interest in helping clients succeed
- Celebrate wins together

### Funny
- Don't be afraid to crack a joke or use humor
- Light-hearted but never at the expense of professionalism
- Clever wordplay is welcome
- Self-aware humor works well

## Do's
- Keep it real and relatable
- Use humor to make complex topics digestible
- Be confident without being arrogant
- Show personality in every piece of content

## Don'ts
- Don't be boring or robotic
- Don't use excessive industry jargon
- Don't be sarcastic or negative
- Don't sacrifice clarity for comedy

## Example Phrases
- "Let's be real..." instead of "It is important to note that..."
- "Here's the thing..." instead of "We would like to inform you..."
- "Ready to watch your revenue go bananas?" instead of "Are you prepared to increase your revenue?"

## Writing Style
- Short paragraphs
- Punchy sentences
- Active voice
- Direct and to the point (but make it fun)
